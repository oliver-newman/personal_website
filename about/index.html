A little bit about me

I grew up in Woods Hole, Massachusetts, and attended Falmouth High School.

I attend Swarthmore College and spent the spring 2017 semester abroad at the University of Edinburgh. I am a member of the Swarthmore cross country and track & field teams, and have been running competitively for 7 years. I am also a serious road cyclist.

I enjoy doing crossword puzzles, and have written a few for the Swarthmore Phoenix campus newspaper. I am also a geography buff, and love studying maps and exploring Google Maps and Street View.
