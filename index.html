I am a junior computer science major at Swarthmore College pursuing a career in software engineering, particularly full-stack development.

About Me

I have a wide variety of interests and have always considered myself a generalist; I also like to have a complete understanding of everything I work with as opposed to blindly accepting various levels of abstraction, hence my desire to be competent with many different levels of the stack.

I have theoretical computer science and math background, but what I lack in hands-on tech industry exposure and software development experience I make up for with a burning desire to learn new technologies, and a passion for creating high-quality products to be used by millions.

I am a staff member of the student-run Swarthmore College Computer Society, where I work mainly with Python, Django, and Unix maintaining a variety of tech-related services for the Swarthmore College community.

I'm a distance runner on the Swarthmore cross country and track and field teams, and an avid road cyclist as well.

In my spare time, I enjoy doing and creating crossword puzzles, and studying geography (i.e., looking at maps and exploring Google Earth).
